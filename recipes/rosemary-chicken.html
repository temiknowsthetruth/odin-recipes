Crispy Rosemary Chicken and Fries

Description

This one-pan crispy rosemary chicken and fries is ready in 15 minutes. Rosemary, garlic, and oregano create a mouth-watering aroma. If using dried herbs in place of fresh, use 1/3 the amount. The potatoes are crispy like French fries; just serve with ketchup. Kids love this!

Ingredients

  • 8 chicken thighs
  • 6 small red potatoes, quartered
  • ½ cup extra-virgin olive oil, or as needed
  • 1 tablespoon chopped rosemary
  • 1 ½ teaspoons chopped fresh oregano
  • 1 ½ teaspoons garlic powder
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Toss chicken and potatoes with oil in a large bowl; transfer to a large baking dish or cookie sheet with sides. Sprinkle with rosemary, oregano, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven, uncovered, for 1 hour; baste during last 15 minutes for extra crispness.
